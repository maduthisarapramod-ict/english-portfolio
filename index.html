Reference: TED Talks Official Portal (Published: April 2018)
Summary:
This presentation features cognitive scientist Lera Boroditsky exploring the direct relationship between native languages and human psychology. She introduces distinct cultural communities (such as the Kuuk Thaayorre) who use absolute cardinal directions (North, South, East, West) instead of relative terms (Left, Right) for spatial awareness.
